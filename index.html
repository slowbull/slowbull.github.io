Zhouyuan Huo

I am a research scientist at Google. I received Ph.D. degree in Electrical & Computer Engineering at University of Pittsburgh under the supervision of Prof. Heng Huang. Before that, I received B.Eng degree at Zhejiang University in 2014. My CV can be found here.

My recent research interest lies at the intersection of machine learning and distributed optimization, especially federated learning and self-supervised learning.
